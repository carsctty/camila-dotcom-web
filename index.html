“What’s Going On” – Marvin Gaye (1971) While some songs age, this one only grows more relevant. Marvin Gaye’s voice floats over soft horns and soulful rhythm, asking hard questions about war, inequality, and peace. It’s both protest and prayer — emotional, thoughtful, and beautifully arranged. Listening today feels like hearing a plea for empathy that the world still needs.
